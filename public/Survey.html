Rank the Projects

Instructions

1. You will begin the survey by ranking the different projects in order from most wanted project to your least wanted project.

2. To view the project information, click on the project title. The difficulty is based on how complex the problem is and not based on the amount of work.

3. Drag and drop the projects in order, and hit the submit button at the bottom when projects are ranked correctly.

4. Please know you are being timed, and you can pause the timer whenever you need to throughout the survey.
