Instructions

1.Based on your previous rankings please add a score (0-10) to each project to determine how much you want each project. 10 being you really want that project compared to the rest, and 0 being you don't want the project.

2.To view the project information again, click on the project title.

3.The score of a project ranked lower must be equal to or less than the project ranked above it.

4.If no score is added, the projects will automatically be scored 0 meaning you have no preference between the projects.
